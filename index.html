        About this tool

        Visualization of developmental expression patterns for subplate-enriched and subplate-specific genes in the mouse. The data is based on microarray and deep-sequencing gene expression studies at embryonic, postanatal and adult stages and public available in situ hybridization studies (see Hoerder-Suabedissen et al., 2012).
